    Welcome to Mangata and Gallo

    We specialize in crafting exquisite jewelry to capture life's most cherished moments. From engagements to weddings and anniversaries, our designs evoke the essence of these significant milestones. Our Austin-based storefront is more than a jewelry store; it's a reflection of passion, creativity, and timeless elegance. Led by Mariana, our owner and lead designer, our pieces seamlessly blend artistry and craftsmanship. Discover our collections, each piece a love-infused masterpiece, whether online or in-store. Join us on this journey of creating enduring treasures that encapsulate life's beauty.

    Our Artisans

    Behind every piece of Mangata and Gallo jewelry is a team of skilled artisans who share Mariana's dedication to excellence. With years of experience and a passion for their craft, our artisans bring her designs to life, ensuring every detail is perfected.

    The Collection

    Our jewelry collection is a celebration of classic elegance and modern sophistication. Each piece is carefully curated to embody the spirit of Mangata and Gallo. From timeless diamond rings to intricate necklaces, our collection offers a range of choices for every special occasion.

    Stay Connected

    Join our community of jewelry enthusiasts and stay updated with the latest from Mangata and Gallo. Follow us on social media to get a glimpse of our behind-the-scenes creative process, learn about upcoming collections, and connect with fellow jewelry lovers.
